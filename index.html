I'm an Atlanta transplant pivoting into tech bringing experience in education, coaching and customer service. I am fascinated by where technology is going, especially in how it relates to music and socioeconomic and political impacts. Ultimately, I want to participate, build and share in the direction that the world is going. Ultimately I hope to take what I learn back to my hometown to build up families like my own in underserved communities from underrepresented demographics. Ideally, that would manifest as a summer program for kids and/or an upskilling program for adults, free of charge.

Outside of tech, I love basketball and have a strong sports background as a former college and short-term professional athlete overseas. While I do miss playing, I love coaching and spent the last year coaching girls basketball at Atlanta International School. I have been very fortunate to teach and learn from some incredible athletes and coaches. In addition, I write music and poetry and am enjoying getting to know the ins and outs of Atlanta.
